## Get Started
1. Get the Model from here
2. Open your desired place and head over to the toolbox
3. From there, head over to your inventory and insert Flux
4. Choose one way to implement Flux in your game
    1. Simply run the game and let Flux setup itself
    2. Ungroup the various models in their respective service instance

---

## Customizing Features
1. Open the Model called `ReplicatedStorage`
2. Open the folder called "drax.flux"
3. Open the Flux module script
At the top of the script, all changeable settings will be listed + a small description.
To fully understand though what each setting does, you should continue reading to the Features section.
